    📊 BATCH COMPARISON

    Compare performance of two batches side by side.

    ⚙ WATER QUALITY THRESHOLDS (per pond)

    Set custom alert thresholds for each pond. Alerts fire when readings go outside these limits.

    📐 CAGE DIMENSIONS (for stocking density)

    Enter cage size once. Stocking density (kg/m³) will auto-calculate for all cages.

    ⚠ Default thresholds: Warn 15 kg/m³ · Danger 25 kg/m³. Configure per-pond in Water Quality Thresholds above.

    🏭 FEED INVENTORY

    Track feed stock per size. Deducted automatically when cage data is saved. Alerts when stock is low.

    🔗 OPS–FINANCE SYNC

    Automatically bridge feed costs and harvest invoices from Operations to the Finance module. Settings are saved to Firebase and shared across all devices.

    Auto-sync feed costs to P&L
    Each feed entry creates a "Feed Cost" expense in Finance P&L for that month
    Auto-create invoice on harvest
    Each harvest record creates a Draft invoice in Finance → Sale Invoices
